CHLLI GROWTH

  1. Seeding

Chili pepper seeds of your preferred variety

Seed trays or small pots with drainage holes

Grow lights or a sunny window

  1. Plant the Seeds:

Fill the seed trays or pots with soil, leaving about half an inch of space at the top.

Plant 2-3 chili seeds in each pot to 1/2 inch, and cover the remaining.

Gently water the soil to settle it around the seeds.

  1. Provide Proper Conditions:

Place the trays or pots in a warm location with temperatures to encourage germination.
